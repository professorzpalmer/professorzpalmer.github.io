Writing for the Web

Cretae Shape Design Share ONLINE

WRT 351 emphasizes learning rhetorical structures best suited for writing for the web. Students practice professional writing as they learn to write web documents for community and commercial internet audiences.

Student Learning Outcomes

  • Analyze a variety of professional writing contexts.
  • Apply user-centered design processes.
  • Develop strategies for presenting effectively.
  • Apply effective strategies for collaborative writing.
  • Recognize the ethical and social responsibilities of professional writers.
  • Discover the range of career opportunities in professional writing.
  • Identify how documents are visually constructed.
  • Create documents that use effective rhetorical strategies for various contexts.
